The primary use of proceeds of the capital raise will go towards aggressively expanding Igloo’s sales and marketing efforts. Related to the rebrand, Harrison said:

Over the past few years we have been so focused on building a great product – we have now done that, so it is time to re-introduce ourselves to the market with a fresh, exciting look. Our name has not changed, but our logo and website reflect a more professional, confident Igloo with ambitions to become the leading ETRM provider in Europe.

The Company already counts some of the largest European hedge funds and physical energy aggregators as clients but is tapping into the growing demand for fast, flexible, cloud-based trading software solutions given the digitalisation of the energy markets. Regarding this opportunity, Hugh Brunswick said, “We see first-hand the shift in the market to a higher frequency, shorter-tenor trading, with customers demanding fully-electronic and direct market access. Igloo is uniquely positioned to capitalize on this opportunity given its state-of-the-art technology and deep European energy market expertise.
